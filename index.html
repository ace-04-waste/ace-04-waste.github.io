....The spark 290620261118

"If we were stranded in a typical place in the universe, we would die almost immediately. But that is because we do not yet know how to live there. A typical cube of space the size of our solar system contains about a million tonnes of matter, mostly hydrogen. With the right knowledge, one could transmute that hydrogen into all the elements one needed to build a space station, and then one could live there, and thrive, and build a civilization." David Deutsch
Two things My life has always been immeasurably changed by knowledge examples include research on hypertrophy , coffee, touring Europe,fixing a floating shelf and tools
The other point is that Life without matter is dull AF. it struck me the first time i read that quote that being irritated by matter on earth makes absolutely no sense when you consider the alternative of sitting in that cube . suddenly everything became interesting and my curiosity shot through the roof.
Knowledge is the lever I use to bend reality - software Matter is the hardware - the lego bricks.
